# Risen Six — Brand Palette

The brand carries gold continuity from the CW Custom Guitars headstock mark, paired with a deep charcoal stage background and a single ember accent for highlights.

## Primary

| Role | Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Phoenix Gold (highlight) | `gold-100` | `#F8DC91` | Top of gradient, light reflections |
| Phoenix Gold (core) | `gold-500` | `#C9962B` | The dominant gold tone |
| Phoenix Gold (deep) | `gold-900` | `#6B4515` | Bottom of gradient, shadow |

The phoenix mark uses a vertical linear gradient: `#F8DC91 → #C9962B → #6B4515`.

## Neutrals

| Role | Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Stage Black | `night-900` | `#0A0A0A` | Hero backgrounds, premium dark mode |
| Charcoal | `night-700` | `#1A1A1A` | Body text on light backgrounds, monochrome mark |
| Cream | `cream-50` | `#FAF6EE` | Light-mode background, monochrome mark on dark |

## Accent

| Role | Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Ember | `ember-500` | `#E25C2B` | CTAs, progress fills, "rise" moments |

## Type stack

- **Display / wordmark:** [Cinzel](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Cinzel) (700) — heraldic, premium, all-caps.
- **Body:** [Inter](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter) (400/500/700) — clean, modern, screen-optimized.
- **Mono / tab notation:** [JetBrains Mono](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/JetBrains+Mono) — fret diagrams, chord charts.

## Tagline

> **Six strings. Endless rise.**

Use sparingly — under the wordmark on the homepage, in email footers, on launch collateral. Don't repeat across every page.
